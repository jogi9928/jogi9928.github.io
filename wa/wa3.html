Big Mountain Skiing

What is Big Mountain?

Big mountain skiing is a style of skiing that involves skiing expert terrain off-piste

Off-piste terrain any slope that is not marked by piste or trail marker poles and has not been prepared by piste grooming machines

Incorporating style and speed, big mountain skiers have revolutionized skiing
